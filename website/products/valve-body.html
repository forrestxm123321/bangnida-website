Key Features

  • ✓ 4-axis milling with live-tool turning — complex valve geometry completed in just two setups
  • ✓ 316 stainless steel construction — excellent corrosion and pitting resistance for hydraulic systems
  • ✓ Cross-drilled internal passages — precision Ø8 mm intersecting fluid channels
  • ✓ Electroless nickel plating — uniform coating on internal and external surfaces for corrosion protection
  • ✓ 3000 PSI rated — 100% hydrostatic pressure tested for guaranteed performance
  • ✓ Six G1/2 threaded ports — precision threaded with class 2A fit for leak-free connections

Application

This hydraulic valve body is engineered for high-pressure fluid control systems in industrial equipment and mobile machinery. The 316 stainless steel substrate provides excellent corrosion resistance in hydraulic fluid environments, while the electroless nickel coating ensures uniform protection on all internal passage surfaces. Cross-drilled ports and precision threaded connections enable reliable manifold assembly for directional control, pressure relief, and flow regulation valves operating at up to 3000 PSI.
