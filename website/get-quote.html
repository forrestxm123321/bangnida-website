File Formats

Accepted CAD File Formats

We accept a wide range of industry-standard CAD formats. Can't find yours? Contact us — we'll find a way.

.stp

STEP

Industry standard for 3D data exchange. Preferred format for complex geometries.

.igs

IGES

Widely supported legacy format. Suitable for surface and wireframe models.

.sldprt

SolidWorks

Native SolidWorks files accepted. Supports both parts and assemblies.

.stl

STL

Mesh-based format. Ideal for organic shapes and complex freeform surfaces.

.dxf

DXF

2D vector format. Best for turned parts, profiles, and flat patterns.

.pdf

PDF Drawing

Detailed engineering drawings with dimensions, tolerances, and notes.

Don't see your format? Email us at info@bangnida.com — we support many additional formats including Parasolid (.x_t, .x_b), Autodesk Inventor (.ipt), and more.
