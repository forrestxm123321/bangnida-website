Industrial

High-Precision Drive Shaft

A stepped drive shaft precision-machined from 4140 alloy steel, heat-treated to HRC 52-55 for high wear resistance. Processed on CNC turning centers with C/Y-axis live tooling for keyway and cross-hole machining, followed by centerless grinding to achieve ±0.005 mm concentricity between all diameters. Threaded ends per class 2A specification.

  • CNC turning with live tooling — keyway & threads in one setup
  • 4140 alloy steel — quenched & tempered to HRC 52-55

  • Centerless ground surface — 0.4 μm Ra finish on bearing journals
