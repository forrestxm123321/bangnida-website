Enhance the appearance, durability, and performance of your machined parts

Surface Finishing for CNC Machined Parts

Surface finishing is a critical step in the CNC machining process that enhances a part's appearance, corrosion resistance, wear resistance, and functional performance. At Bangnida, we offer a comprehensive range of finishing options to meet your exact requirements, from standard as-machined surfaces to specialized coatings.

Our Finishing Services

Anodizing (Type II & Type III)

Suitable for aluminum parts. Type II (decorative) provides color options and moderate corrosion resistance. Type III (hard coat) provides exceptional wear resistance for demanding applications. Available colors: clear, black, red, blue, green, gold, and custom.

Powder Coating

Durable, impact-resistant finish in any RAL color. Ideal for outdoor components and parts requiring a thick protective layer. Thickness: 60-120 μm.

Electroless Nickel Plating

Uniform coating on complex geometries including internal surfaces. Excellent corrosion resistance and hardness (up to 58 HRC after heat treatment).

Passivation

Chemical treatment for stainless steel that removes free iron and enhances natural corrosion resistance. Required for medical, food, and pharmaceutical applications.

Bead Blasting

Uniform matte surface finish. Removes tool marks and creates a consistent appearance. Glass bead or aluminum oxide media available.
