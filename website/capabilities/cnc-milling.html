Precision CNC Milling Services

3-axis, 4-axis & 5-axis milling with tight tolerances and rapid turnaround

What is CNC Milling?

CNC milling is a subtractive manufacturing process that uses computer-controlled rotating cutting tools to remove material from a workpiece. It is one of the most versatile and widely used precision machining processes, capable of producing complex geometries with exceptional accuracy and surface finish.

At Bangnida, we operate over 95 CNC milling machines including 3-axis, 4-axis, and full 5-axis machining centers from leading manufacturers such as Mazak, DMG MORI, Haas, and Fanuc. Our experienced programmers and machinists handle everything from simple prototype parts to complex production runs.

Our CNC Milling Capabilities

3-Axis CNC Milling

Ideal for prismatic parts, 2D and 2.5D geometries. Our 3-axis mills handle the majority of standard machining tasks with high efficiency and precision.

4-Axis CNC Milling

With an additional rotary axis (A or B), 4-axis milling enables machining of multiple faces in a single setup — reducing lead time and improving accuracy for complex parts.

5-Axis CNC Milling

Full 5-axis simultaneous machining allows us to produce complex organic shapes, undercuts, and deep cavities in a single setup. Ideal for aerospace, medical, and automotive components. Learn more about 5-axis machining →
