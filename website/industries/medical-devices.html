Medical CNC Machining Services

ISO 13485-compliant precision components for the medical device industry

Precision Machining for Medical Device Manufacturing

The medical device industry demands the highest standards of precision, cleanliness, and regulatory compliance. Every component must meet stringent FDA and ISO requirements while delivering reliable performance in life-critical applications. At Bangnida, we provide ISO 13485-aligned CNC machining services for medical device manufacturers worldwide.

Our facility houses 200+ advanced CNC machines including 5-axis machining centers, Swiss-type lathes, and precision turning centers capable of holding tolerances to ±0.005 mm. We specialize in machining medical-grade materials including stainless steel (303, 304, 316L, 17-4 PH), titanium (Grade 2, Grade 5 Ti-6Al-4V), PEEK, and implant-grade polymers.

Medical Components We Machine

Surgical Instruments

We manufacture a wide range of surgical instruments including forceps, scissors, clamps, retractors, needle holders, and custom surgical tooling. Our precision machining ensures proper fit, smooth operation, and reliable performance. We work with 300-series stainless steel, titanium, and specialty alloys to meet biocompatibility and sterilization requirements.

Orthopedic Implants

We produce orthopedic implant components including knee and hip replacement parts, bone screws, plates, spinal implants, and dental abutments. These components require exceptional surface finish, tight dimensional control, and material purity. Our quality system ensures full traceability and compliance with implant-grade material specifications.

Diagnostic Equipment Parts

Medical diagnostic equipment relies on precision-machined components for accurate operation. We machine parts for CT scanners, MRI systems, ultrasound devices, blood analyzers, and patient monitoring systems. Our capabilities include complex enclosures, precision manifolds, sensor housings, and connector components.

Drug Delivery Systems

We precision machine components for drug delivery devices including insulin pens, auto-injectors, inhalers, and infusion pump parts. These components require tight tolerances for proper sealing and dose accuracy. We work with medical-grade plastics and stainless steel to meet biocompatibility standards.

Micro Components & Implantable Devices

Our Swiss-type lathes and micro-machining centers enable production of ultra-precision miniature components for implantable devices and micro-surgical instruments. Parts as small as Ø0.5 mm can be produced with features held to ±0.005 mm tolerances.

ISO 13485 & Regulatory Compliance

Our quality management system is aligned with ISO 13485 standards for medical device manufacturing. Key elements of our medical quality process include:

  • Device history record (DHR) for complete traceability
  • Risk management per ISO 14971 principles
  • Cleanroom-compatible manufacturing protocols
  • 100% dimensional inspection with CMM and vision systems
  • Surface finish measurement (Ra, Rz) for implant-grade parts
  • Material certifications with mill traceability

Surface Finishing for Medical Devices

We provide surface treatments that meet medical device requirements for biocompatibility, cleanability, and corrosion resistance:

  • Electropolishing for stainless steel (per ASTM B912)
  • Passivation per ASTM A967
  • Glass bead blasting for uniform matte finish
  • Micro-bead blasting for implant surfaces
  • Anodizing for titanium (per ASTM B136)
  • PTFE and Parylene coating for lubricity
